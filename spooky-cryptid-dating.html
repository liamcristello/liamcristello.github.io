About The Process

This project was my introduction to the Ren'Py game engine, as well as my first time programming in Python. Thankfully, both were less complicated than I had initially feared, and I learned a lot in a short period. For a few of our project members, this was their first game jam, and I really enjoyed being a part of their introduction to one of my favorite extracurricular activities! Ren'Py was a very different game engine from ones I had worked with previously (Unity and Unreal), but I really enjoyed the variety, and appreciated how well-designed it was for this genre of game. Because it was made in a shorter timeframe, with a team that was scattered across different schools and with tight schedules, the game is currently unfinished. However, we have been talking about continuing its development, which I would love to stay a part of!
