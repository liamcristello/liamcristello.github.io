PONG & OBSTACLES

About The Game

A 3D Virtual Reality game for use with the HTC Vive and Wii Balance Board, developed during my co-op internship at Northeastern's ReGame-VR Lab. Play a version of the classic game Pong, but with a twist: your paddle is controlled by shifting your weight on the Wii Balance Board. As the difficulty increases, more challenges will take effect, such as decreased paddle size, more balls to bounce, or even obstacles in 3-D space which you must deflect with the Vive controllers!

Unfortunately, because this is a research game designed for in-house use, I cannot provide a download link.

About The Process

This is one of two projects I've been working on for my co-op internship, the other one being Throw At Target. As with that game, it is designed for physical therapy research, in this case to help define the optimal challenge level for engaging children in research tasks. Also like Throw At Target, I am the only dedicated project member, so I work largely independently. I meet with my supervisor weekly to discuss goals and set deadlines, but when challenges crop up I have to take the initiative to solve them myself. I have been fortunate enough to be connected to different sources where I can ask questions, which is very helpful since this co-op is my first time working solo with VR technology. It has been a very rewarding process, for all its challenges.
