About The Game

A 2D shoot-'em-up game where you use the keyboard and mouse to dodge bullets and fire back bullets of your own. Pick up power-ups to earn bonuses, such as a protective shield or rapid-fire mode! Fight multiple different bosses, each with their own unique attack pattern as you try to survive.

About The Process

This was a game I made for my final project in my Game Programming class. It was a semester-long project, in-between regular assignments for the class. I worked with two other programmers to make this game. We wanted to make a rogue-like game in the vein of Enter the Gungeon or The Binding of Isaac, but for scope purposes we ended up focusing on the boss fights. The playtests we ran with fellow classmates gave us a great idea of what we were doing well and where we could improve, and we took that feedback to heart when iterating on our design. I was put in charge of the User Interface and User Experience features, since we were told initially that our game could do a better job of communicating information to the player. I revamped our UI/UX, and our future playtests went better since we had improved on the requested features. It was a fun project to work on, and a nice way to wrap up the course!
